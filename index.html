Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I get a response after submitting an inquiry? +
We respond within 15 minutes during business hours (8am–9pm, 7 days a week). Outside those hours, you'll hear from us first thing in the morning.
Is there a fee for using StayMatch? +
Our matching service is completely free for guests. We work with BnB hosts who pay us a small finder's fee — so you get great options at no extra cost to you.
How do you verify the properties on your network? +
Every property is physically visited by our team before being listed. We verify ownership, condition, photos, and amenities. We do periodic re-checks to maintain our standards.
What locations in Kenya do you cover? +
We currently cover Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Nanyuki, Diani, Malindi, Naivasha, Eldoret, and Nyeri. We're growing fast — ask us if your location isn't listed.
What payment methods do hosts accept? +
Most hosts accept M-Pesa and bank transfers. Some accept cash. We'll include payment details in the options we send you.
Can I request a BnB for a group or event? +
Absolutely! We specialise in group stays, corporate retreats, birthday getaways, and family trips. Just note it in your inquiry and we'll find the perfect fit.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help you with anything not covered here. Reach out directly on WhatsApp or send us an email and we'll get back to you quickly.
